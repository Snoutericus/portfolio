Cybersecurity consultant with a focus on defensive practices to protect the confidentiality, integrity and availability of information.

Skills

  • Phishing Analysis

    Analyzing emails, looking for malicious artifacts, and taking defensive actions based on findings.

  • Threat Intelligence

    Knowledge of different threat actors such as hackers and APTS, their motivations, and using tools such as MISP to research these threats. Understanding the different forms of intelligence (operational, tactical, and strategic).

  • Digital Forensics

    Applying science to analyze evidence of cyberattacks to use in prosecution of threat actors.

  • SIEM Technologies

    Gathering information related to security events from various sources using Splunk

  • Incident Response

    Reducing damage and achieving recovery in the lowest timeframe possible by following the four phases of Incident Response:

    • Preparation
    • Detection and Analysis
    • Containment, Eradication, & Recovery
    • Post-incident Response
